Trincomalee Beach Retreat

4 Days / 3 Nights | Beach & Culture

From: USD $420 per person

Tour Summary

Relax on the golden beaches of Trincomalee, explore ancient temples, enjoy whale watching, and soak up the laid-back coastal lifestyle.

Highlights
  • Nilaveli & Uppuveli beaches
  • Koneswaram Temple
  • Pigeon Island snorkeling
  • Whale & dolphin watching
Tour Themes

Beach · Culture · Relaxation

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival & Beach Relaxation

Arrive in Trincomalee, check into your beachside hotel, and enjoy the evening by the shore at Nilaveli or Uppuveli Beach.

Day 2 – Pigeon Island & Snorkeling

Take a boat trip to Pigeon Island National Park for snorkeling among coral reefs and colorful marine life. Relax by the beach in the evening.

Day 3 – Cultural Exploration

Visit Koneswaram Temple, Lover’s Leap viewpoint, and explore the cultural side of Trincomalee. Optional whale & dolphin watching excursion.

Day 4 – Departure

Spend a relaxing morning on the beach before your departure transfer. End of tour.
