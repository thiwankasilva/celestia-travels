Yala Wildlife Safari

2 Days / 1 Night | Wildlife & Adventure

From: USD $349 per person

Tour Summary

Experience the thrill of Sri Lanka’s premier national park, home to leopards, elephants, sloth bears, and an incredible diversity of wildlife.

Highlights
  • Evening and morning jeep safaris
  • Chance to spot leopards
  • Birdwatching in lagoons
  • Stay in a safari lodge or camp
Tour Themes

Wildlife · Adventure · Nature

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival & Evening Safari

Arrive at Yala, check into your lodge or camp, and embark on your first evening jeep safari in the park.

Day 2 – Morning Safari & Departure

Rise early for a thrilling morning safari. Spot leopards, elephants, and diverse birdlife before departure.
