A 14-day scenic road trip across Sri Lanka — coastlines, tea country, wildlife & culture

Tour Summary

Experience authentic village life, meet warm locals, and explore diverse landscapes — from golden coastlines and lush tea estates to tranquil wildlife sanctuaries. This is a tailor-made itinerary designed for families, couples and friends.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01 — Negombo

Arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport ...

Negombo Highlights
  • Negombo Fish Market
  • Hamilton Canal walks
Day 02 & 03 — Anuradhapura

After a road briefing and vehicle checks, travel along the coastal route (short scenic legs) towards Chilaw and onward to Anuradhapura. Explore the UNESCO-listed sites, stupas, ancient temples and the sacred Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi. Option to visit Mihintale for sunset views.

Activities
  • Cycle-briefing → replaced by driver/vehicle briefing and orientation
  • Heritage sightseeing: Ruwanwelisaya, Thuparamaya, Isurumuniya
  • Mihintale optional excursion
Day 04 — Sigiriya

Drive into the Cultural Triangle and visit Sigiriya Rock Fortress. Option for a Minneriya safari (elephant gathering) if timing aligns. Evenings at a comfortable hotel in the area.

Day 05 & 06 — Kandy

Travel to Kandy, visit Nalanda Gedige and a spice/herbal garden, experience the Temple of the Tooth and cultural dance performances. Leisure time in Kandy’s botanical gardens.

Day 07 — Hatton (Hill Country)

Transfer into the hill country with a scenic train segment option (Kandy → Watagoda), then continue by road through tea plantations with stops at viewpoints and waterfalls (St. Clair’s, Devon). Enjoy highland scenery and local tea culture.

Day 08 & 09 — Yala National Park

Drive southeast to Yala region. Enjoy jeep safaris in Yala National Park to spot leopards, elephants and diverse birdlife; visit Kataragama if included. (Note: some hill segments may be challenging so your driver will adapt the route for safety).

Day 10 & 11 — Mirissa

Head to Mirissa for beach time and optional whale-watching (seasonal). Relax on golden sands, explore nearby fishing villages (Kirinda) and Bundala wetlands en route.

Down South Highlights
  • Coconut Tree Hill
  • Secret Beach Mirissa
  • Surfing at Weligama
  • Hiriketiya Beach
  • Coffee at KAI Ahangama
  • Blue Beach Island
Day 12 — Galle

Coastal drive to Galle with a walking tour of the Royal Dutch Fort, boutique shops and sunset views at the ramparts. Consider a Madu River safari if time allows.

Day 13 — Colombo

Drive to Colombo for urban highlights: Lotus Tower (panoramic views), Fort district, Pettah markets and city shopping. Overnight in Colombo.

Day 14 — Departure

Transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport for your departure. End of services.
