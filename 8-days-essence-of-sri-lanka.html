8 Days – Essence of Sri Lanka

Explore heritage, wildlife, hills, and coast in one immersive journey

Duration: 8 Days / 7 Nights From: USD $800 per person.

Tour Summary

Embark on a curated road-trip through Sri Lanka’s iconic highlights—from the rock fortress of Sigiriya to the misty hills of Nuwara Eliya, the wildlife-rich Yala, the serene beaches of Bentota, and the urban charm of Colombo.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Sigiriya

Arrival and airport assistance. Head to Sigiriya and visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage en route. Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress and Pidurangala for panoramic views. Explore a nearby village and enjoy a traditional boat ride, followed by a wildlife safari in Minneriya National Park.

Day 2 – Kandy

After breakfast, proceed to Kandy via Dambulla Cave Temple. Enjoy a spice garden visit, then explore the Temple of the Tooth, Royal Botanical Gardens, and a cultural performance in the evening.

Day 3 – Nuwara Eliya

Travel to Nuwara Eliya with stops at Ramboda Waterfall, Seetha Amman Kovil, and a tea garden. Enjoy high tea at Grand Hotel and a stroll around Gregory Lake.

Day 4 – Yala

After breakfast, drive to Yala. Journey includes scenic train ride (option) through Ella and Nine Arches Bridge. Embark on a 3–4 hour wildlife safari in Yala National Park.

Days 5 & 6 – Bentota

Drive to Bentota and discover stilt fishermen, explore Galle Fort, and enjoy a boat safari on Madu River. On Day 6, visit Lunuganga Estate and participate in water sports or a turtle hatchery visit.

Day 7 – Colombo

Proceed to Colombo, visit Gangaramaya Temple, explore Independence Square and local shopping, and stroll along Galle Face Green and Old Parliament area.

Day 8 – Departure

Transfer to the airport for your departure. End of services and memories to last a lifetime.
