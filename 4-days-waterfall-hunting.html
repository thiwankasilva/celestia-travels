4 Days – Waterfall Hunting in Hill Country

Discover Sri Lanka’s most breathtaking waterfalls in one scenic journey

Duration: 4 Days / 3 Nights From: USD $400 per person.

Tour Summary

This short but spectacular journey takes you deep into Sri Lanka’s central highlands to witness some of the island’s most iconic waterfalls. From Kithulgala’s hidden gems to the misty cascades of Nuwara Eliya, Haputale, and Ratnapura, this 4-day tour is designed for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts alike.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Ginigathhena

Begin your adventure at Sandun Falls in Kithulgala, followed by a visit to the mighty Aberdeen Falls. Spend the night near Aberdeen Falls surrounded by nature’s beauty.

Day 2 – Nuwara Eliya

Journey to Nuwara Eliya with stops at Laxapana Falls, St. Clair’s Falls, and Devon Falls. Evening leisure in the cool hill station atmosphere of Nuwara Eliya.

Day 3 – Ramboda to Haputale

Explore Ramboda Falls before continuing your journey to Haputale, a quiet mountain town with stunning landscapes. Overnight in Haputale.

Day 4 – Haputale to Airport

Visit Bambarakanda Falls, Sri Lanka’s tallest waterfall, followed by the magnificent Diyaluma Falls. Conclude the tour with a scenic return journey.
