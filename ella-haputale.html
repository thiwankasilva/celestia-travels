Ella & Haputale Scenic Escape

3 Days / 2 Nights | Adventure & Relaxation

From: USD $350 per person

Tour Summary

Explore the beauty of Sri Lanka’s hill country with misty mountains, waterfalls, and lush tea plantations. This tour combines nature, adventure, and tranquility in Ella and Haputale.

Highlights
  • Ravana Falls & Nine Arches Bridge
  • Scenic train ride through the hills
  • Tea plantation & factory visits
  • Lipton’s Seat viewpoint
Tour Themes

Nature · Adventure · Relaxation

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival in Ella

Arrive in Ella, visit Ravana Falls, and take a leisurely evening walk to the famous Nine Arches Bridge. Overnight stay in Ella.

Day 2 – Scenic Train & Tea Experience

Enjoy a scenic train ride through the highlands, explore lush tea plantations, and visit a working tea factory. Overnight stay in Ella.

Day 3 – Haputale & Departure

Travel to Haputale, enjoy the breathtaking view from Lipton’s Seat, and relax before departure. End of tour.
