Sigiriya Rock Fortress Tour

2 Days / 1 Night | Cultural

From: USD $249 per person

Tour Summary

Climb the magnificent Sigiriya Rock, admire ancient frescoes and royal gardens, and explore the cultural triangle with a blend of heritage and village experiences.

Highlights
  • Sigiriya Hike
  • Visit Dambulla Cave Temple
  • Traditional village experience
  • Overnight in Sigiriya/Habarana
Tour Themes

Culture · Heritage · Adventure

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Dambulla & Village Experience

Transfer to Sigiriya, visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, and enjoy a traditional village tour with catamaran ride. Overnight stay in Sigiriya/Habarana.

Day 2 – Sigiriya Climb & Departure

Early morning climb of Sigiriya Rock Fortress followed by a visit to a herbal garden before return transfer.
